Project :

Customer Purchase Pattern Analysis Scenario: The business wants to optimize its inventory by understanding which products are purchased frequently by different customer demographics.
Problem Statement: Finding insights into customer purchase trends to improve stock management and targeted marketing.

Questions to Solve:

1.	Which age group makes the most purchases?

2.	What is the most popular product category for different age groups?

3.	Which gender has the highest purchase count on average?

4.	Find customers who made more than 20 purchases and their common product category.

5.	Create a bar chart of product categories vs. total purchases.

6.	Analyze seasonal variations in purchase count if applicable.
